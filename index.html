About SkySpace

SkySpace was first founded in 2011 by Sergine Adelina. Previously, the founder was a writer who published her books in several publishers. After several years, Sergine decided to establish a company in the publishing sector.

At the start of the company, Sergine built a reading house next to the publishing center considering the lack of libraries around where she lived.

Apparently, the reading house she created attracted many fans. The reading house also developed into a publishing center which eventually became known to many people.

Now, the company has 53 branches spread throughout Indonesia and a total of more than 11,000 partners who have joined. Apart from that, this company also publishes six different types of books.

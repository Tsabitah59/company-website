There are already more than 60 authors who have trusted us to publish their work. Be one of them by fulfilling the applicable terms and conditions.

Terms and Conditions

  1. The manuscript you send must be 100 to 200 pages long.
  2. If you send a manuscript for a children's book, especially a serial book manuscript, complete the manuscript with illustrations and a story concept.
  3. The manuscript is typed in 12 pt font size and 1.5 spacing.
  4. You can write a script with any theme as long as it doesn't offend SARA and isn't vulgar.
  5. Include a story summary/synopsis about the script you wrote.
  6. Don't forget to include the type of manuscript you are writing (fiction, nonfiction, juvenile, etc.)
